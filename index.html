Core Mechanics

INFORMATION IS POWER

No wallhacks, no minimaps. Every piece of information is earned — through listening, watching, and deceiving.

Focus Mode

Freeze in place and perceive sound waves through walls. See footsteps, loot interactions, and disturbances — but you can't move while listening.

Lootmarks

Carrying stolen loot leaves traces. Run and every step betrays you. Sneak and you might just slip away — but every second counts.

Sabotage

Thieves can disable guard task groups — cut cables, jam signals. Guards must find and remove the sabotage before they can progress.

Distractors

Grab a brick, hurl it at a car alarm or a glass panel. Create noise to lure guards away — or bluff them into thinking you did.

Prey & Predator

Spotted? The chase begins. Thieves gain a speed burst, guards get a highlight on the target. Break line of sight to end the hunt — parkour is your escape.

Hiding Spots

Slip into wardrobes, crates, barrels. Peek out to gather intel. But guards can search hiding spots — and if they find you, it's over.
